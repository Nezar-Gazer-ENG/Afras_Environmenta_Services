About Us

Afras is proud to be a customer-focused company, with an established track record of successful projects, which have helped many public and private organizations achieve their goals

Afras was set up in 1976 to compete for building, and hospital operation and maintenance contracts in the central region of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Since then, we’ve expanded to cover a range of business areas, including hospitality, security, pest control, housekeeping and cleaning, and landscaping and commercial gardening.

Not only are we highly regarded by our clients, we also enjoy classification grades in nine areas from the Ministry of Municipal and Rural Affairs Contractors Classification Agency. For example we hold Grade 1 classification in both the Maintenance of Buildings and the Maintenance of Medical Centers.

We’ve also expanded into other regions: starting with a further branch in the western region in Jeddah, we then opened offices in Dammam, Taif, and most recently Makkah. Our plan is to open further Afras branches to improve our service throughout the Kingdom.

Our clients rate us highly, and make Afras their first choice for many reasons. High on the list comes our commitment to quality, our flexibility, and our competitiveness. Clients have made us aware of their appreciation in our constant development services, and our people. They are also fond of how attentive we are to their requests then rigorously comply with their requirements and specifications.

Afras is proud to be a customer-focused company, with an established track record of successful projects, which have helped many public and private organizations achieve their goals.

We’ve developed our Environmental Services over many years, and introduced techniques to make sure very high cleaning and hygienic standards are maintained in all kinds of situations, from clinics to universities and civic buildings. What’s more, our teams are managed by qualified administrative and supervision staff who are adept at organising service schedules, so you to get the very best from your outsourced Environmental Services. When you choose Afras, you also benefit from the peace of mind of working with a company which listens, which delivers great service, and which works as a partner to ensure your Environmental Services needs are fully met.
